Customizing 404 Error Pages

MojoMotor's "404 Page not found" page will be generated by the system any time a page is requested that doesn't exist. The page is generated from a template that can be found at system/mojomotor/errors/error_404.php. This page can be altered to meet your needs, but it is highly recommended leave the 2 variables $heading and $message in place.

This page is not otherwise a part of MojoMotor, and cannot accept Mojotags or other system resources. It must be straight HTML/CSS and PHP.
